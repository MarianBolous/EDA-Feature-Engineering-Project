## Data Story & Key Insights

### Funding & Location Trends  
- California dominates** startup funding, followed by New York & Massachusetts.  
- Massachusetts startups receive the highest average funding per round.  

### Startup Age & Success  
- Startups that raise funding sooner tend to survive longer**.  
- Those that struggle to raise capital for years often fail faster.  

### Founder Count & Success 
- Startups with 2-3 founders have the highest chances of survival.  
- Single-founder startups struggle, while teams of 5+ founders show mixed success.  

### Investor Influence 
- Well-connected investors (who invested in successful startups) increase survival odds.  
- Some investors are strongly linked to failed startups, indicating risky investments.  

### NLP Analysis on Startup Descriptions

- Topic Modeling grouped startups into 5 main themes, such as Cloud Computing, AI, Marketplaces, & Enterprise SaaS.  

- High-frequency words in successful startups: "platform," "user," "solution," "technology."  

- Failed startups tend to use "idea," "market," "product," suggesting less traction-driven focus.
